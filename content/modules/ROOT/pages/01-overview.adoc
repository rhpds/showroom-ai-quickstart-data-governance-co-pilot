== Your role and mission

You're a data architect at TradeNexus, a fast-growing e-commerce company, and you've been assigned to evaluate AI-powered analytics for an important compliance initiative.

TradeNexus needs to enable governance-aware conversational analytics on their relational e-commerce data for regulatory requirements. Your manager has explained: "We need to give business users the ability to query our data naturally while ensuring we never expose PII or violate data retention policies -- this is critical for our upcoming compliance audit."

**Your assignment**: Evaluate the Data Governance Copilot quickstart to determine if it can enable self-service analytics while maintaining strict data governance controls for TradeNexus.

**The objective**: Demonstrate that AI-powered analytics can be both powerful and compliant, allowing business users to ask questions naturally while the system automatically enforces governance policies.

== Success criteria for your mission

By the end of this workshop, you'll have practical skills to address TradeNexus's governance-aware analytics requirements:

* Understand agentic AI fundamentals -- the agentic loop, Model Context Protocol (MCP), and how this differs from chatbot-style AI → **recognize when agentic AI is the right architectural choice**
* Learn key design decisions when building agentic AI applications → **make informed architectural choices for your use cases**
* Explore the Data Governance Copilot architecture -- backend orchestrator, MCP server, inference components → **understand how the pieces fit together**
* Evaluate orchestration choices between custom implementation and Llama Stack → **choose the right approach for your requirements**
* Experience hands-on governance-aware analytics with TradeNexus data → **see how natural language queries respect data policies**
* Understand data sovereignty benefits of running AI on OpenShift → **keep sensitive data and AI processing within your controlled infrastructure**

**Technical outcome**: You'll have hands-on experience with agentic AI that directly applies to TradeNexus's need for compliant, self-service analytics.

**Business benefit**: A proven approach to democratize data access while maintaining governance controls, reducing both analyst workload and compliance risk.

== TradeNexus's analytics challenges

**The situation**: TradeNexus needs to enable self-service analytics for business teams while ensuring strict compliance with data governance policies for an upcoming regulatory audit.

**Project timeline**: Evaluate solutions and provide recommendations within the current quarter to support the compliance initiative.

**Current challenges** (operational pain points):

* **Manual data access requests**: Business analysts must submit tickets to data teams for every query → 2-3 day turnaround slows decision-making and creates bottlenecks
* **Governance knowledge scattered**: Data classification policies live in PDFs and wikis → analysts don't know which tables are safe to query or which contain PII
* **SQL skill barrier**: Not all stakeholders can write SQL → limited access to insights for non-technical users
* **Compliance risk**: Without automated governance checks → risk of accidentally exposing PII or querying deprecated datasets in reports

**The opportunity**: Agentic AI with Model Context Protocol can provide natural language access to data while automatically enforcing governance rules, and you've been selected to evaluate its feasibility for TradeNexus's compliance requirements.

**Technical perspective**: "We need AI that doesn't just answer questions -- it needs to understand our data governance policies and actively prevent policy violations. Traditional chatbots just generate SQL; we need an agent that verifies certifications, checks for PII, and respects retention policies before executing anything."

== Your vision of success

If the Data Governance Copilot proves effective for TradeNexus's use case, here's the potential impact:

**Immediate improvements** (this quarter):
* **Self-service analytics**: Business users ask questions in natural language → answers in seconds instead of days
* **Automated governance**: The copilot checks policies before every query → eliminates manual governance verification and reduces compliance risk

**Strategic benefits** (within 6 months):
* **Faster decision-making**: Analysts get insights 10x faster → improved responsiveness to market changes
* **Reduced analyst workload**: Data team focuses on strategic work instead of routine queries → better use of scarce data engineering resources
* **Audit-ready compliance**: Complete query logs with governance validation → demonstrate compliance controls to auditors with full transparency

**Success metric**: A demonstrable improvement in query turnaround time (days to seconds) while maintaining 100% policy compliance, presented to stakeholders as a viable solution for TradeNexus's regulatory requirements.
